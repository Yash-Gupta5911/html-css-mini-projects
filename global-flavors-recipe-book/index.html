Italian — Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti 200g
  • Eggs 2
  • Pancetta 100g
  • Parmesan 50g

Steps

  1. Boil pasta.
  2. Cook pancetta.
  3. Mix eggs + cheese and combine.
  4. Serve hot.

Tip: Reserve pasta water to loosen sauce.

Asian — Stir-Fry Veg Noodles

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Mixed veg
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Prep veg.
  2. Stir-fry on high heat.
  3. Add noodles & sauce.

Tip: Keep heat high for wok flavor.
